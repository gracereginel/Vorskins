ABOUT US

VOrskins is a care and beauty e-commerce, a place where you can
get various products that can nourish your skin. You can find local
brands that sell skin care and body care products that are vegan,
cruelty-free, and use non-toxic ingredients with affordable prices.

Vorskins is a care and beauty e-commerce that was created in 2019
because of the many obstacles experienced by the Indonesian
people when they wanted skin care and beauty products from
official distributors and registered with BPOM.

VOrskins’ vision is to make VOrskins the number 1 most complete
and trusted e-commerce for care and beauty products in Indonesia.
While VOrskins’ mission is to expand the care and beauty industry
in Indonesia to be even better.
